About Us





Java Beans is a group of people who are deeply passionate about coffee. We want to spread our love and passion for coffee and let people learn and be a part of the growing coffee culture. In order to make this happen, our goal is to make the highest quality coffee beans and equipment available and accessible to everyone. We want people to make and experience coffee at its finest.


We have partnered with the leading coffee traders and equipment manufacturers in the world and we're bringing them to you! Shop and enjoy different varieties of coffee beans hailing from coffee growing regions like Brazil, Vietnam, Colombia, Indonesia and Ethiopia. Pick your preferred type of coffee beans, your favorite roasts, experiment with different coffee blends or enjoy single origin coffee. Make that perfect cup of coffee with equipment from established brands like Hario, Chemex and Aeropress. Looking to open your own cafe? Get your equipment from us!



Mission


Our mission is to make high quality coffee beans and equipment available and accessible to consumers

Vision


Our vision is to increase growth of coffee culture in the Philippines and make more people aware that they can make great coffee on their own and experience it at the comfort of their home or office.
